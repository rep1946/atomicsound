About the studio

Atomic Sound is a state of the art sound studio that combines the asethitic and warmth of analog sound with the technological innovation of today. Founded inn 2013, we restored the historic Red Hook firehouse of Engine Company 2 to a high end recording sound replete with a 800 square foot live room with 16 foot pitched ceilings, 4 isolation booths, top of the line vintage and brand new gear and instruments, microphones and a vast array of analog and digital outboard gear. Our Neve VR60 console along with 2 EMT plate reverb units along with a wide variety of class A microphone pre-amps, Studer and MCI/Sony 2" analog tape machines and the highest resolution Avid-based pro tools rig will bring your next recording to next level. This is a full service tracking and mixing facility that crafts and engineers the best sound available.   View the floorplan
